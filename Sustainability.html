Driving to a more sustainable
future



We all have a choice when it comes to where we want to get and how we get there. That includes Audi. That’s why sustainable mobility is our
clear path ahead.









Letting it go



Maisie Williams behind the wheel of the Audi e-tron
Sportback leaves the past behind and takes the road to a
sustainable future.




Our path



Audi has a clear vision: to reduce CO₂ emissions and become carbon-neutral in balance as a company by 2050. To get there, we’re letting go of
the things holding us back and re imagining how we bring our vehicles to the road – from the supply chain, to the emissions at our production
plants, to the design of our models’ entire life cycle.





Value Creation & Production



We at Audi are constantly trying to find ways to do business more sustainably. That means not being afraid to rethink everything that goes into an Audi model and what kind of cars we put on the road.


Talking Business



Our Brussels plant is already CO₂-neutral in balance¹, and we aim for all Audi production facilities globally to be the same within five years.


Digitization



The ideas we have are only as good as the people they come from, so we at Audi are committed to bringing together the brightest minds to shape the future of mobility.
